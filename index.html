Dungeon Escape!

Explore the dungeon, find the key, run away from the monsters, and escape!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move your hero.
  • Find one of the two keys hidden in three chests.
  • Avoid the monsters roaming the dungeon.
  • Open a locked door with your key to escape.
  • If a monster catches you, you lose!
  • Game built with Phaser 3.90
  • Map personally designed with Tiled
  • Sprites & tiles from various free assets websites
  • Fonts by Google Fonts
  • UI styled with Bootstrap 5
