How to Play

Desktop Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your hero. Hold a direction to keep moving. The hero stops when you release the key.

Mobile Controls

Tap above, below, to the left, to the right, or in a diagonal direction from the hero to move. The hero will walk toward where you tap. You can go straight or diagonal.

Game Rules

Find one of the two keys hidden in three chests.

Avoid the monsters roaming the dungeon.

Open a locked door with your key to escape.

If a monster catches you, you lose!
